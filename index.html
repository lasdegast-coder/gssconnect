List Of Events

We believe going to networking events is the way to go. Conferences, presentations, expositions, fairs etc. Talk to people, get inspired and learn real-life stuff. We created a WhatsApp group where we send the most interesting events.
  • Impact Fair. 3-day networking event with dozens of impact-driven companies. Many of them are open to hiring. 5 stages with speakers all day.
  • National Climate Expo. Around 100 companies and organisations in the climate and energy sector display themselves.
  • Sustainability Career Event. Focused on students and young professionals.
  • Social Impact Factory. An organisation in Utrecht organising small-scale, short events, on many diverse subjects such as social entrepreneurship with a networking component.
  • Utrecht Union. They invite very prominent speakers on all kinds of subjects. More lecture-style.
  • Upstream. Targeted to startups and investors. You can watch dozens of sustainability-focused startups pitch, as well as many other speakers.
  • Change now. Huge event in Paris with companies, ngo's and initiatives on the widest range of sustainability topics. You can volunteer and enter for free. It's in april, we drive there with an Utrecht crew.
